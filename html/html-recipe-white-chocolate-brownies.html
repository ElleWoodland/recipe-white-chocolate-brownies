White Chocolate Brownies

A.K.A. Blondie Brownies

This recipe is written by kdahlman, and found on https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/217158/white-chocolate-blondie-brownies/

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 3 chopped squares of white chocolate (1 ounce each)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of white chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F/ 175 degrees C.
  2. Grease the saucepan.
  3. Melt butter and white chocolate together in the saucepan on low heat. Remove from heat to cool slightly.
  4. Mix eggs, sugar, and vanilla extract in the mixing bowl.
  5. After eggs are beaten, mix in the butter mixture from the saucepan, flour, and salt until completely combined.
  6. Scoop the batter into a well-prepared baking pan and sprinkle with white chocolate chips.
  7. Bake in the oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of the batter comes out clean (approximately 30-35 minutes).
  8. After baking is done, let sit for about 10 minutes before cutting into bars.
